Prioritising locations for street tree planting in Newark, NJ

Street trees are a form of 'green infrastructure' with many benefits, particularly in terms of absorbing stormwater runoff

The U.S. Forest service and partners have created a tool called iTree to help users determine the benefits of planting trees and prioritise locations where trees will have the biggest impact. iTree creates a 'priority planting index' based on user-selected criteria.

This map shows locations where flooding has been reported along with the iTree results for prioritising census block groups according to these criteria and weights:

- high imperviousness - 40%
- low tree canopy per capita - 30%
- low tree stocking level - 30%
